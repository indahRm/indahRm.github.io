User Documentation

Expanded version of the skills test I completed to join the nonprofit Organization for Transformative Works (OTW) as a Documentation Writer. I edited the document after joining to follow OTW's style and writing guidelines. The purpose of this tutorial is to instruct users on how to post a new work to OTW's literary site, Archive of Our Own (AO3). The text was written:

  • For beginner site users who may be unfamiliar with site features.
  • Ensuring both computer and mobile device users could follow the tutorial.
  • To take into account that a user might be utilizing a screen reader or other accessibility device.

My tutorial links to other documentation pages on AO3, as is standard. I have also styled the format to mimic AO3's interface, which is available open source.
